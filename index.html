Thanks for making your way to my website. Briefly, I am a biostatistician who just thought it might be worthwhile creating a little website to give you a sense of who I am. I had never met any Black biostatisticians when I started pursuing my doctoral studies in biostatistics. I also didn't meet a Black, female, lesbian biostatistician, like myself, until after I graduated. So to all the other Black or female or LGBTQ+ students and professionals in STEM fields, if you are wondering where are the others like you, here I am. And I am one of many many more who are doing everything we can to increase the sense of belonging, scientific identity, and success of underrepresented groups in STEM. I want you to know that you will make amazing contributions to science and it is your diversity and brilliance that is going to change the world. Much respect!
